About Us

Who We Are

The Marasy Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit organization that works for you, the local business owner. Our mission is to support our members and promote a community of cooperation. We advocate for our business members by working with them to navigate the challenging waters of owning and operating a business in Abu Dhabi. We are one of the most effective Chambers in Abu Dhabi at delivering benefits to our members and the community.

What We Can Do For You

Although there are many reasons to support the Marasy Chamber, a lot of our members join for many networking and advertising opportunities. Some join for the special discounts we offer our members of the chamber. And some join for the many valuable services we offer including help with licensing, relocation, and helping navigate labor issues. The benefits of membership are endless.

What We Do For Marasy

In addition to what we can do for your business, the Marasy Chamber of Commerce works behind the scenes to provide many of the activities that bring residents and visitors to our area. We also work on economic development and tourism to create a community we can all be proud of.
