Demographics

  • Who: Moms and other adults who are responsible for getting kids ready for outdoor sports
  • Age: 20 to 45
  • Family: At least one child aged 3 to 18 who participates in outdoor sports
  • Technology: Users want information quickly and are familiar with using a smart phone and the internet.

Personas

Fictional Name: Jackie Smith
Family: Single Mom of 2 boys ages 4 and 6.
Background: Grew up playing soccer and loves to watch her boys dominate on the field.
Education: College Degree
Job: 4th grade teacher
Goals: Wants to be the best mom she can but has her hands full supporting the family by herself.
Interests: Loves playing sports and being active with her kids.

Fictional Name: Sarah Jones
Family: Married with 4 kids, 2 boys and 2 girls, ages 1-8.
Background: Was a dancer growing up but married a jock.
Education: Graduated High School, Some College
Job: Stay-at-Home mom
Goals: Keeping her busy family organized and making sure everyone is where they need to be and taken care of, while managing the home and meals.
Interests: Family, cooking, reading, and sleeping when she can.

Scenarios

  • It’s Saturday morning and there are blue skies outside but I need to know if my kids will need long sleeves under their jerseys.
  • It’s Tuesday and the kids have soccer practice tonight, it’s been raining all day, is it going to stop?
  • Mom is out of town and Dad needs to get the kids ready for soccer, how hot is it? Should he pack extra water?
  • I’m going to watch my 5-year-old niece play soccer, should I bring an Umbrella?
  • Is there a chance of lightning during the game today, the game might be cancelled.
